About
SAP Navigator
A search engine built for SAP professionals — no logins, no subscriptions, no waiting.

As an IT consultant working daily with SAP, I kept running into the same frustrating problem: knowing roughly what I needed to do, but spending too much time hunting down the right transaction code or table name. Documentation is scattered, the built-in search is limited, and asking a colleague breaks their flow too.

So I built SAP Navigator — describe a task in plain language, enter a code directly, or browse structured troubleshooting flows. Everything runs locally in your browser.

Transactions
Plain-language search across 16,000+ SAP transaction codes with ranked results and match reasons.
Tables
Look up SAP standard tables by name or keyword — key fields, purpose, and related tables included.
Decode
Describe a symptom or error — get the likely cause, a starting transaction, and numbered next steps.
Flows
Step-by-step process guides by module. Star the flows you use most — they stay pinned at the top.

No backend · No tracking · No account needed · Runs entirely in your browser

Privacy Policy
Privacy Policy
Last updated April 2026
tl;dr We collect nothing. No analytics, no cookies, no servers. The only data stored is on your own device — your starred flows and theme preference.
1

No Data Collection

SAP Navigator does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There is no backend, no analytics, no cookies, and no tracking of any kind. It is a fully static site hosted on GitHub Pages.

2

What Stays on Your Device

The only data written to your browser is stored in localStorage on your own device:

sap-nav-favorites Your starred flow IDs
sap-nav-theme Light or dark mode preference

This data never leaves your device. Clear it any time via your browser's site data settings.

3

Search Input

Everything you type into the search boxes is processed entirely within your browser. It is never sent to a server, never logged, and never persisted beyond your current session.

4
