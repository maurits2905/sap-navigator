About
SAP Navigator
A search engine built for SAP professionals — no logins, no subscriptions, no waiting.

As an IT consultant working daily with SAP, I kept running into the same frustrating problem: knowing roughly what I needed to do, but spending too much time hunting down the right transaction code or table name. Documentation is scattered, the built-in search is limited, and asking a colleague breaks their flow too.

So I built SAP Navigator — describe a task in plain language, enter a code directly, or browse structured troubleshooting flows. Everything runs locally in your browser.

Transactions
Plain-language search across 16,000+ SAP transaction codes with ranked results and match reasons.
Tables
Look up SAP standard tables by name or keyword — key fields, purpose, and related tables included.
Decode
Describe a symptom or error — get the likely cause, a starting transaction, and numbered next steps.
Flows
Step-by-step process guides by module. Star the flows you use most — they stay pinned at the top.

No backend · No tracking · No account needed · Runs entirely in your browser

Search Operators
*
Wildcard — e.g. VA* starts with VA, *vendor* contains "vendor"
?
Single character — e.g. SU0? matches SU01, SU02…
" "
Exact phrase — e.g. "goods receipt"
OR
Either term — e.g. SU01 OR PFCG
NOT
Exclude — e.g. vendor NOT payment

No Personal Data Collected

SAP Navigator does not collect, process, store, or transmit any personal data whatsoever. There is no backend server, no database, no user accounts, no analytics, no cookies, and no tracking pixels of any kind. The site is fully static — every computation happens locally in your browser and nothing leaves your device.

2

What Stays on Your Device

The only data written by SAP Navigator is stored in your browser's localStorage — a local storage mechanism that never syncs to any server. The following keys may be written:

sap-nav-favorites The IDs of flows you have starred
sap-nav-theme Your light or dark mode preference

This data is stored solely to improve your experience across sessions. It is never transmitted anywhere. You can delete it at any time via your browser's site data or storage settings.

3

Everything you type into the search fields, every result you view, and every interaction you make with the tool is processed exclusively within your browser. None of this is logged, captured, transmitted to a server, or persisted in any form after you close the tab. There is no mechanism by which the author can observe your usage.
